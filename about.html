About the Python Class

The FICA Python Class is a vibrant and inspiring group of learners passionate about exploring the digital world. From Python programming and robotics to Scratch, PictoBlox, web design, and graphic design, they take themselves in the art of creating and innovating. Through hands on projects like the Grade Calculator, they work on real world challenges, mastering both coding and design. Each session is a journey writing Python scripts, programming robots, crafting interactive animations, and building beautiful websites. This community of curious minds is more than just a class but its a launchpad for future developers, designers, and innovators driven by creativity, teamwork, and the desire to turn ideas into reality.

The class is led by the passionate and skilled Eng. Andrew, who not only guides their technical learning but also mentors them with strong Christian values and teamwork. Under his leadership, the class thrives on collaboration, curiosity, and creativity.
